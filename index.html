Here at Munder Difflin Paper when a special project needs a unique paper, we use our years of experience to create the best possiblesheet. The client may specify size, shape, thickness, surface, color, watermarks, and just about anythingelse. Inclusions such as metallic threads, plant fibers or wool may be added to the paper for visualtexture. You may want to choose a color from the large variety of colors we have created in the past, or we can make a new one especially for your project.
